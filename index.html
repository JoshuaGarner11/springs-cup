RULES · FORMAT · WHAT TO BRING

01 · WHO PLAYS

MS + HS Students

Open to middle school and high school students from any church or youth group across the Springs. You don't have to attend Springs Church to play.

02 · SQUAD SIZE

6 on the Roster · 4 on the Court

Up to 6 players per squad. Minimum 4 to compete. If you don't have 6, that's fine — sign up with what you've got and we'll find you a game.

03 · ENTRY

$25 / squad

Covers the gym, refs, balls, and pizza after. Paid through Springs Church (CCB) when you register. Squads aren't locked in until payment clears.

04 · FORMAT

Round Robin → Single Elim

Pool play first — every squad gets a few games. Then single-elimination bracket for the squads that earn it.

05 · WHAT TO BRING

Yourself, Mostly

  • ▸ Athletic shoes (no boots / sandals)
  • ▸ Squad-color shirts if you want (optional, looks 🔥 in photos)
  • ▸ Water bottle
  • ▸ One adult leader from your church / group
06 · RULES OF WAR

Standard Dodgeball

  • ▸ Hit below the shoulders = you're out
  • ▸ Catch a throw = thrower's out, plus a teammate back in
  • ▸ No headshots on purpose. Keep it fun
  • ▸ Refs' calls are final. Argue and you sit
07 · QUESTIONS?

Hit Up Anthem Youth

Email joshua@springs.church with anything. We'll get you sorted.
